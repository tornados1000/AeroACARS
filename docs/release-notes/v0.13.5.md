## 🇬🇧 English

**v0.13.5 — small but important auto-file fix for pilots who like to switch tabs before PIREP submission.**

### What was broken

When you switched to another tab than "Cockpit" before the PIREP submission — for example to **"Landing"** to look at your landing first — the PIREP was **NOT** automatically submitted. You had to switch back to the Cockpit tab for auto-file to trigger.

### What happens now

Auto-file now runs **tab-independent** in the background. After touchdown you can freely switch between tabs (look at the landing, check stats, whatever) — as soon as the flight reaches phase "arrived", the PIREP gets submitted automatically.

### What does NOT change

- Auto-file toggle in Settings → Filing still works as before (on/off per pilot)
- On divert detection auto-file still waits for your divert decision (won't silently file with the wrong destination)
- On a sim crash with the Stream F resume banner you still have to operate the 3-button dialog yourself — that's a user interaction, not an auto job

### What you need to do

Nothing. Auto-updater rolls out v0.13.5, everything is active on next app start.

### Reminder about today's server-side update (v0.13.4)

In case you missed it — the validator was significantly relaxed this morning: practically every flight is now trusted and auto-accepted. Only two specific triggers still land in the VA owner's review queue (PIREP with no touchdown / sim crash signature). In-flight banners still show anomalies, **but no longer affect your verdict**. If you fly normally, you won't notice the integrity system at all.
